In command mode
:w save
:w! force sace
:q quit
:q! force quit
:w b.php save as
:set nu (show line number)
:set nonu (show line number)
:set tabstop =2 (change defaut tab space)
:set autoindent
:/word (find a word)
:n1,n2s/search/replace/g (from line n1 to line n2, search and replace globally)
:u (undo)
:dd (cut)
:yy (copy)
:3dd (cut 3 lines)
:p (paste)
:r filename (load file)
:sp (open a new window)
:%y (yank all)

In normal mode
yim (yank a word)
